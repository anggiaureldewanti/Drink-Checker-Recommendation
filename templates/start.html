Drink Checker App

About This App

This app helps you to find the perfect drink based on your preferences. You can select the temperature, base, and taste, and we'll recommend drinks that match your choices.

How to Use

Follow these simple steps:

  1. Click the "Start" button above to begin.
  2. Select your preferred temperature for the drink (hot or iced).
  3. Choose the base ingredient (coffee, tea, milk, mojito).
  4. Select your preferred taste (sweet, bitter, sour).
  5. View the recommendations based on your selections.
